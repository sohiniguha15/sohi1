Baggage

New Times Airlines offers cabin baggage allowances for domestic and international travel on all its flights. DISTRIBUTION For Boeing and Airbus aircraft, the dimensions are 55 cm (22 inches) + 35 cm (14 inches) + 25 cm (10 inches). HE NUMBER OF PIECES AND THE WEIGHT AREA OF THE ECONOMY Only 01 piece of hand baggage having a maximum weight of 8 kg is permitted. Effective 01st November 2019, excess weight over and above the allowed free hand baggage allowance of 8 kg will be charged at INR 500 /kg ( GST extra) for Domestic Sectors. For travel ex and into India to US/UK/EUR/Australia/Canada charges are USD40/kg (GST extra). All other international categories for travel into and out of India will be charged USD 15/kg (plus GST). Maximum permissible hand baggage after paying excess charges must not exceed 10 kg including Duty-Free items. If your handbag weighs more than 10 kg, it will not be allowed in the cabin and has to be checked in as registered baggage.Read more
